OUR MISSION

The mission of Three Sisters Gardens is to teach youth how to grow, harvest, and distribute organic vegetables right where we live. We are based in traditional Native American values of respecting Mother Earth and all living things.

At Three Sisters Gardens, we are using urban agriculture to engage community members to get involved in creating a food system that is good for us and the environment.
